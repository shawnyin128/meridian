## What To Remember

Gao et al. - 2025 - Soft Adaptive Policy Optimization: We propose Soft Adaptive Policy Optimization (SAPO), which replaces hard clipping with a smooth, temperature-controlled gate that adaptively atten- uates off-policy updates while preserving useful learning signals. When a sequence contains a few highly off-policy tokens, GSPO suppresses all gradients for that sequence, whereas SAPO selectively down-weights only the offending tokens and preserves the learning signal from the near-on-policy ones, improving sample efficiency. SAPO weights token-level updates by a bounded, sigmoid-shaped function of the importance ratio, centered at the on-policy point.

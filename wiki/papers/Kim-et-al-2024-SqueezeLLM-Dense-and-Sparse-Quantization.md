- Purpose: Allocates non-uniform quantization centroids according to both weight distribution and sensitivity, so low-bit dense weights preserve model outputs better than uniform bins.

### Dense-and-sparse decomposition

- Purpose: Splits the weight matrix into a dense quantized part plus a tiny FP16 sparse part containing outlier or highly sensitive values, reducing dense quantization range while keeping overhead low.
- Operates on: weight matrix; outlier thresholds; sensitivity mask; sparse storage format.
- Produces: dense quantized matrix; FP16 sparse matrix; overlappable dense+sparse inference path.

### Dual transformation for massive and normal outliers

- Purpose: Combines block-wise rotation with zigzag permutation to distribute massive and normal activation outliers across blocks, then absorbs the inverse transform into weights before low-bit quantization.
- Operates on: activation matrix with massive/normal outliers; weight matrix; block rotation; zigzag permutation.

## Mechanism Details To Verify

- `problem_decomposition` / DuQuant: DuQuant distinguishes normal outliers from massive outliers; the method exists because SmoothQuant-like smoothing can fail on rare extremely large activations. Provenance: p. 1 / Abstract.
- `equivalent_transform` / DuQuant dual transform: DuQuant uses an invertible transform G built from block rotation and permutation, then quantizes (XG)(G^-1 W); the transform should be checked as an equivalence before quantization. Provenance: p. 2.

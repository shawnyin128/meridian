## What To Remember

Schulman et al. - 2018 - High-Dimensional Continuous Control Using Generalized Advantage Estimation: We present experimental results on a number of highly challenging 3D locomotion tasks, where we show that our approach can learn complex gaits using high-dimensional, general purpose neural network function approximators for both the policy and the value function, each with over 104 parameters.

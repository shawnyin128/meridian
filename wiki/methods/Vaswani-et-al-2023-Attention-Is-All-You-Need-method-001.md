# Vaswani et al. - 2023 - Attention Is All You Need

- Source paper: [[papers/Vaswani-et-al-2023-Attention-Is-All-You-Need|Vaswani et al. - 2023 - Attention Is All You Need]]
- Summary: We propose a new simple network architecture, the Transformer, based solely on attention mechanisms, dispensing with recurrence and convolutions entirely. In this work we propose the Transformer, a model architecture eschewing recurrence and instead relying entirely on an attention mechanism to draw global dependencies between input and output. The best performing models also connect the encoder and decoder through an attention mechanism.
- Inputs: token embeddings, query/key/value projections, positional or attention bias terms, model weights
- Outputs: contextual token representations, sequence-model predictions
- Assumptions: attention computation and positional representation capture the dependencies required by the sequence task
